Suckers for good coffee

The June Sucker is a fish species native to Utah (specifically Utah Lake and Provo River). It is uniquely ours, but due to ecosystem alteration and mismanagement, June Suckers have become endangered. Now, thanks to targeted research and conservation efforts, populations of this local treasure are beginning to recover. We are June Sucker Coffee simply because the June Sucker represents our commitment to our local community. We wish to provide customers a responsible, conscious cup of coffee by highlighting locally sourced and ethically produced goods, and by doing our part to protect, conserve, and enhance the vibrancy of our local environment and community.
